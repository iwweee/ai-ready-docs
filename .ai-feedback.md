## 🛠 How to use this loop:
1. **AI Detects**: While processing a task, the AI notices a doc is vague or wrong.
2. **AI Logs**: The AI writes a new entry to this table.
3. **Human Reviews**: A human developer checks `.ai-feedback.md` periodically.
4. **Human Fixes**: The human updates the target `.md` file.
5. **Human Clears**: Once fixed, the log entry is moved to an archive or deleted.
